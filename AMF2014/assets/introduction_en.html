The Taiwan MICE industry is once again welcoming its premier annual event! The Asian MICE Forum, known as the AMF, will take place from September 17 to 18, 2014. Featuring leading international MICE experts and information about the latest industry trends, the Forum will notably have as guest speakers the organizers of the Tokyo International Marathon and of the Sydney Festival to share their first-hand experiences and tips in organizing world-class events. The 2014 Asian MICE Forum is expected to convey a new wave of enthusiasm and ideas to Taiwan’s MICE scene.

Held by the Taiwan External Trade Development Council (TAITRA) and commissioned by the Bureau of Foreign Trade, Ministry of Economic Affairs, the AMF is currently entering its ninth edition. To increase the scope of the event and inspire Taiwan’s MICE practitioners, this year’s Forum will bring together Mr. Paul Woodward, managing director of the Global Association of the Exhibition Industry (UFI); Mr. David DuBois, President of the International Association of Exhibitions and Events (IAEE); and Mr. Martin Winter, board member of the International Congress and Convention Association (ICCA) Asia Pacific, for a CEO discussion. Hot topics such as “innovation and storytelling” and “innovative destination marketing” are also on the agenda, plus featured on the list of guest speakers are winners of the 2014 UFI Operations & Services Award, the 2013 SITE Crystal Awards, and the 2013 ICCA Best Marketing Award, each willing to share their experience and secrets to success.

Date: September 17-18, 2013 (Wednesday - Thursday)
Venue: Taipei International Convention Center (TICC)
